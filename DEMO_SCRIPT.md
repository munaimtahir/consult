# Hospital Consult System - Demo Script

## Introduction (1 minute)
*   **Presenter:** "Good morning/afternoon, everyone. Today, I'm excited to present the Hospital Consult System, a powerful tool designed to streamline and modernize the inter-departmental consultation process in a hospital setting."
*   **Key Message:** The current system of phone calls and pagers is inefficient and prone to errors. Our solution provides a centralized, real-time platform for managing consults, improving communication, and ultimately, enhancing patient care.

## Login & Dashboard (1 minute)
*   **Action:** The presenter logs in using their email and password.
*   **Presenter:** "The application uses a standard email and password for secure authentication. Once logged in, clinicians are taken directly to the consults list, their central hub for managing all their consultation requests."
*   **Key Feature:** The consults list provides a comprehensive overview of all consults, with intuitive filtering options to help clinicians quickly find the information they need.

## Creating a New Consult (2 minutes)
*   **Action:** The presenter clicks the "+ New Consult" button to open the new consult form.
*   **Presenter:** "Let's create a new consult. The form is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, guiding the clinician through the process of providing all the necessary information."
*   **Action:** The presenter fills out the form, highlighting the following key fields:
    *   **Patient Information:** Search for a patient by MRN.
    *   **Consult Details:** Select the requesting and target departments, and assign a priority level (Routine, Urgent, or Emergency).
    *   **Clinical Information:** Provide a reason for the consult, a specific clinical question, and any relevant patient history.
*   **Presenter:** "By capturing all this information upfront, we can ensure that the consulting physician has everything they need to provide a timely and effective consultation."
*   **Action:** The presenter submits the form.

## Managing a Consult (3 minutes)
*   **Action:** The presenter navigates to the newly created consult in the consults list.
*   **Presenter:** "Now, let's look at the consult from the perspective of the consulting physician. They can view all the details of the consult, including the patient's information, the reason for the consult, and the clinical question."
*   **Action:** The presenter demonstrates the following actions:
    *   **Adding a Note:** Add a progress note to the consult, demonstrating how clinicians can communicate and collaborate within the application.
    *   **Changing the Status:** Change the status of the consult from "Pending" to "In Progress" and then to "Completed," showing the entire lifecycle of a consult.
    *   **Final Note & Recommendations:** Add a final note with recommendations, demonstrating how the consulting physician can provide their expert opinion and guidance.
*   **Presenter:** "The system tracks the entire history of the consult, providing a complete audit trail of all actions and communications."

## Filtering & Searching (1 minute)
*   **Action:** The presenter returns to the consults list.
*   **Presenter:** "The consults list is designed to be highly interactive. Clinicians can easily filter the list by status, urgency, or department, allowing them to focus on the consults that require their immediate attention."
*   **Action:** The presenter demonstrates the filtering and searching functionality, showing how to find specific consults quickly and easily.
*   **Presenter:** "The 'Clear Filters' button provides a simple way to reset the view and see all consults again."

## Conclusion (1 minute)
*   **Presenter:** "The Hospital Consult System is a powerful tool that has the potential to revolutionize the way hospitals manage inter-departmental consultations. By providing a centralized, real-time platform for communication and collaboration, we can improve efficiency, reduce errors, and ultimately, enhance patient care."
*   **Call to Action:** "Thank you for your time. I'd be happy to answer any questions you may have."
